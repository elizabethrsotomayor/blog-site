About Dean

Dean Albertson is a fearless fashion icon, graduate of NYC's Fashion Institute of Technology, and the visionary behind Style Slate. With a keen eye for trends and a rebellious spirit, he effortlessly blends high-end luxury with street-style edge. Dean's blog is a curated collection of his personal style, outfit inspiration, and behind-the-scenes glimpses into the glamorous world of fashion. When he’s not hunting down the next big thing, you can find him exploring hidden boutiques, attending fashion shows, or simply inspiring his followers to embrace their individuality.
